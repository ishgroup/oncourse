Luxury accommodation in Kangaroo Valley

Drive just two hours south of Sydney to swap the concrete for bush and enjoy the serenity of Kangaroo Valley.

Arundel is 6km from Kangaroo Valley township and just 2km from Kangaroo Valley Country Club and Golf Course. Take a leisurely drive over the mountains to visit the nearby towns of Berry, Gerringong and Kiama on the coast or head up to the highlands and enjoy the historic towns of Berrima, Bowral, Mittagong and Bundanoon.

Arundel is a working Arabian Horse Stud bordered by Morton National Park.
